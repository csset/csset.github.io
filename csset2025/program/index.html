Symposium Program

Thursday, Apr 18, 2025. Room # 1203

9:00 am - 9:15 am Welcome Note-- Dr. Tanvir Chowdhury
9:15 am - 10:15 am
10:15 am - 11:55 am Poster Presentation
The poster presentation & judging is expected to begin at 10:15. We will have over 20 students presenting their research and creative work. The project presentation will be judged based on Judging Rubric, and winning presentations will be announced on in the closing ceremony.

Every poster and presentation will be presented in the Arthur Weisberg Family Applied Engineering Complex. From 10:15 am - 12:00 pm (EST), presenters will present their posters in front of judges. During this time period, judges will ask questions and evaluate presenters’ responses.
12:00 am - 12:30 pm Closing Note-- Dr. Tanvir Chowdhury
